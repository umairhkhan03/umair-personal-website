---
title: Ghalib - the Conqueror
date: 2025-01-01
language: Urdu
---

Aik baat aisi jo chand lafzon mai kehdi,

ussey chaand bhi dohraata raha.



aik nazar aisi jo chand lamho ki thi,

ussey chaand bhi chahta raha.



aik aahat jo dil se mehekti nilki,

usey chaand bhi lehraata raha.



aik roshan-si jugnu… woh roshan-sa chehra

na chaand… na ghaalib, 

ussey koi nahi bhula saka.
