---
title: dreams in a coffin
date: 2023-01-01
language: Urdu
---

Teri yaad ko mai apne dil se utaar na saka,

in lafzon me un khayaloon ko utaaar na saka.

Un khuwaabon ko jab kitaboon mai in alfaazon se likha,

in kitaboon ko us almaari ke aik koney mai chupaya,

in khwaabon ko mere dil ke aik koney mai dafan kardiya.

ye dil jab aik din zindagi ki rozzamarii mai gum gaya, 

to us dil ke koney ki khayaal ne usey ujaala de diya.
