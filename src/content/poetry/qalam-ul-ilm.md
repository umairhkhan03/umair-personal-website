---
title: Qalam-ul-ilm
date: 2024-01-01
language: Urdu
---

Apne haathon se aik Qalam uthata hai Insaan, 

Aur in Khayalon ki lakeeron se, 

Apne zameer ke kagaz pe likh deta hai Insaan. 

Apni rooh rukhsat karke doosron ke kagaz mai karta hai karaar,

Dil mai basne ka kisko hai ikhtiyaar.



Ijazat diijiye mai hota hoon rawaan,

Ijazat di hai, haath uthaiye to zara,

Ijazat di hai, mere naam ka qalam chalaiye to zara.
