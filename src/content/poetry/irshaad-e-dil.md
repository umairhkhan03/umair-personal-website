---
title: Irshaad-e-dil
date: 2024-01-01
language: Urdu
---

Irshaad-e-dil, 

ye khayalon ki lakeeren,

Mujh pe koi nazm na likh den. 



Chal para pahaaron mai akela mai, 

Sar taaj-o-khazaney ki talash mai. 



Saalon ki Yeh doori, meeloon ka faasla,

Magar Ye kambakht pyaar ki boonde

Is dil ke patthar se tera naqsh mitaati nahi.
